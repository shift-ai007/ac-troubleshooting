# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving this troubleshooting guide.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a branch for your change
3. Make your edits
4. Submit a pull request with a description of what you changed and why

## What We're Looking For

- Corrections to technical information
- Additional troubleshooting steps we missed
- Region-specific tips (we focus on South Florida, but other hot/humid climates face similar issues)
- Clearer explanations or better formatting
- Additional flowcharts for uncommon problems

## Guidelines

- Write for homeowners, not technicians. Avoid jargon or explain it when used.
- Be specific. "Check the filter" is better than "perform routine maintenance."
- Safety first. Never suggest a homeowner do something that requires licensing or poses injury risk.
- Keep it practical. Every recommendation should be actionable.

## About This Project

This guide is maintained by [AC Repair Today](https://ac-repair.today), a licensed HVAC contractor in South Florida. We created it because informed homeowners make better decisions about their cooling systems.
