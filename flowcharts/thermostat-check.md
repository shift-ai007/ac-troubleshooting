## Common Thermostat Settings Mistakes

| Setting | Problem | Fix |
|---------|---------|-----|
| FAN ON | Blower runs 24/7, wastes energy, hides cooling cycle issues | Switch to AUTO |
| HEAT mode | System blows warm air even when it's hot outside | Switch to COOL |
| Schedule off | Away/eco mode stuck, holding temp at 80°F+ | Override or adjust schedule |
| Set too high | "Cold air" feels warm because room is 78°F | Lower setpoint to test |
| No hold | Program changes at 9am, user expects it cool at 10am | Use Hold/Permanent Hold |

## When to Upgrade

Consider replacing your thermostat if:
- It's over 10 years old
- It's a manual dial type (no digital display)
- You can't set separate day/night schedules
- You want smart features (WiFi control, geofencing, usage tracking)
